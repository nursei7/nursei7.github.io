Together We Strive

Zunair, Nurgazy, Usman and Sony are students at NYU Abu Dhabi. Back in pre-Covid-19 times they were “gym buddies”. It was always Sony who gathered all of them for a workout and was guiding his friends showing right techniques and making a training plan.

Unfortunately, quarantine and long distance between them has put their friendship and a healthy habit of exercising together in a hard condition. Each and every one of them are occupied by their hobbies to cope with isolation albeit they miss their friends and the joy of physical activities.

Finally, Sony cannot bear the lonely workouts and longing for seeing his friends. He calls them to invite to a virtual workout where they reunite doing push ups during which everybody catches themselves thinking how important it is to maintain connection with friends and constantly exercise.
