Meme- an image (or multiple moving ones-a video) that has been copied and spread after being transformed from its original state. Whether the transformation is a simple caption or a distortion of the image, its presence is what elevates an image to the status of a meme.

This is a journey to meet three different people and learn from their experiences with memes. Helping me in this journey is Dr. Wojak Kowalski- the Head of Department of Meme Culture and GenZ Studies at the Memebridge School of Arts and Humanities, Yes Chad- A GenZ self-proclaimed Memelord and chief editor for Meme Insider, and finally Incel Boomer- a father of 4 and proud Monster Energy Zero Ultra addict.
